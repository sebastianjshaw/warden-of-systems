Chapter 38 — The Attempt

Alone with Merlin while Elira scouts ahead, you try to summon the Sight on purpose—concentration, emotion, the sword, the trees. Every attempt fails. You conclude it's a reflex tied to danger to those you love, not a skill you can develop. Then Merlin leads you to an ancient carved stone slab in the forest. When you touch it, you feel a faint current and the barest hint of the geometry. Merlin's chest glows brighter there; he seems to belong to such places.
